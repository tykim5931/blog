Hello, I'm Taeyun Kim!

I am an undergraduate student majoring in Computer Science & Engineering at the College of Transdisciplinary Studies in DGIST, Korea.
As a passionate and driven prospective researcher in the field of 3D vision and graphics with AI, I am currently studying innovative ways to reconstruct fine-grained 3D models from 2D images.

My aim is to contribute to creating a future where 3D data is easily accessible and can be utilized to its full potential, just as how data has evolved from text to voice, images, and videos. I strongly believe that advancements in the format of data will shape the way humans think, interact and solve problems, and I am eager to be part of this transformative and dynamic field.
